About CnPack

Background and Target

Delphi/C++Builder/BDS/Rad Studio is famed for its completely open VCL framework: by using third party's codes and components, software developers can improve their productivity enormously. However, among all the third party components, the number of those written by Chinese developers (leave out commercial products) can be counted on the fingers of one hand, let alone those that are comparable to JCL, RxLib, GExperts.

CnPack is aimed to be an excellent large package at the very outset with many people's contribution. It is also intended to provide a great number of codes and documents for Delphi developers' reference and study.

 

Distribution and License

CnPack is an open source project. You can use and copy it freely for non-commercial purposes.

 

Contents

Distinguishing itself from other likenesses, apart from including plenty of components, CnPack also integrates IDE Wizards, property editor, component editor, public forms library, version control and other auxiliary tools. To be detailed, the following is a part of CnPack:

  • Non-visual tools set;
  • User interface control;
  • Database and Report tool;
  • Network communication component;
  • Localized extensive property editor and component editor;
  • IDE Wizards;
  • CnVCS version control system;
  • Lots of reusable form template library.


CnPack is still under development with some functionality to be further implemented. Besides its developers' endeavor, it is your keen support that could make CnPack better. So if you have any concern or suggestion during your use of CnPack, please do not hesitate to contact us. And your contribution will be appreciated.

 

About CnPack Team

CnPack is developed completely by a virtual team over Internet. The team is composed of a group of Chinese fans of Delphi/C++Builder, who volunteer to join together to build an truly excellent third party free package. CnPack becomes Embarcadero Technology Partner(ETP) in 2009.

There are currently more than 250 members enlisted. They are divided into package design group, implementation group, assistant group and website maintenance group, with different responsibilities shared by each group.

As members of CnPack team, we hope not only create an outstanding work, but also more importantly build the best open source developer team in China via the development practice.

CnPack team welcomes all volunteers who are keen to software development to join. It is eligible to make an application once the following requirements are fulfilled:

  • Being energetic and responsible, willing to contribute to Chinese open source development;
  • Have some experience in software development or website maintenance;
  • Have enough time and energy, given that normal study and work is not disturbed;
  • Preferably have a computer of your own with Internet connected.

For more details about the application, please visit our website.
